SNAKE!

How To Play:

Goal:

Get the highest score possible by eating apples to get longer.
Be careful, if you run into the wall or your tail it's game over!

Controls:

Use WASD or "Arrow Keys" to move.
Press 'T' to turn on camera tracking.
Press 'R' to Restart.
Press 'P' to Reset Score And Preferences.
If you want to change settings mid-game you will need to end your current game. you can do this in the settings menu.

Tips:

Your score increases with each apple eaten.
Your score increases faster as the snake gets longer
Your score increases more slowly the more apples there are on the map
